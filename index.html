Why Mangroves?

Cause they actually are the lungs of our planet. Storing more carbon than any other forest on Earth. They are wetland ecosystems protecting coastlines from high waves during storms. Cleaning the water and giving home to hundreds if not thousands of endangered species like the Three-toed Sloth and the Mangrove Hummingbird.
